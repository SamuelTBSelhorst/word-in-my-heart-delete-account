Delete Your Account - Your App Name

⚠️ Warning: This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Account information (email / login details)
  • All notes and cloud backup data
  • Any associated stored app data

How to delete your account

  1. Open the app
  2. Go to the Cloud / Account section (green tick icon)
  3. Tap “Delete Account”
  4. Confirm deletion

Confirmation

After completing deletion in the app, your account and all data will be permanently removed.

You will see this confirmation message in the app:

“Your account has been removed.”
